Quick Summary: Cradle+ helps pregnant women scan products safely. We only collect the minimum data needed to provide our service, never sell your information, and you control your data at all times.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Pregnancy stage, due date (optional), and preferences
  • Scan History: Products you've scanned and saved (stored locally on your device)
  • Support Communications: Messages you send to our support team

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature usage, crash reports (via Firebase)
  • Device Information: Device model, iOS version, app version
  • Product Scans: Barcode numbers for ingredient analysis (processed, not stored)

1.3 Camera Access

  • Camera access is used solely for barcode scanning
  • No photos or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted
  • Camera data is processed locally on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functions

  • Analyze product ingredients for pregnancy safety
  • Provide personalized safety recommendations
  • Maintain your scan history and preferences
  • Enable AI-powered Pregnancy Pal chat assistance

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Improve ingredient analysis accuracy
  • Enhance app performance and user experience
  • Develop new safety features
  • Provide customer support

2.3 Communications

  • Send important app updates and safety alerts
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Notify you about subscription status (if applicable)

3. Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share data in the limited circumstances described below.

3.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, analytics, and crash reporting
  • DeepSeek AI: Ingredient analysis (anonymized barcode data only)
  • Apple: App Store subscriptions and payments

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, such as:

  • Responding to legal processes or government requests
  • Protecting the safety of our users or the public
  • Preventing fraud or abuse of our service

3.3 Business Transfers

If Cradle+ is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Local Storage: Scan history is stored locally on your device
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular Security Reviews: We regularly assess and update our security practices

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access your profile and scan history within the app
  • Update Information: Modify your profile and preferences anytime
  • Delete Scans: Remove individual scans or clear your entire history
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and associated data

5.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional emails (support emails may still be sent)
  • Disable push notifications in your device settings
  • Control analytics sharing in app settings

5.3 Data Portability

You can export your scan history and profile data by contacting our support team.

6. Children's Privacy

Cradle+ is designed for pregnant individuals (typically 18+) and those planning pregnancy. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

7. International Users

Cradle+ is operated from the United States. If you use our service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US, which may have different privacy laws than your country.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our mobile app does not use cookies. We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage, which you can opt out of in the app settings.

9. Third-Party Services

9.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Used for: User authentication, analytics, crash reporting
  • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

9.2 DeepSeek AI

  • Used for: Ingredient safety analysis
  • Data shared: Anonymized ingredient lists only
  • Privacy Policy: platform.deepseek.com/privacy

10. Medical Disclaimer

Important: Cradle+ is for informational purposes only and is not intended to replace professional medical advice. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making decisions about product use during pregnancy.

  • Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance

13. State-Specific Rights

13.1 California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

13.2 European Users (GDPR)

European users have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
